About Me

Hi, I'm Alex. With over 10 years of professional photography experience, I specialize in capturing authentic moments that tell your unique story.

My approach combines technical expertise with artistic vision to create images that are both beautiful and meaningful. I believe the best photos come from genuine connections and natural moments.

When I'm not behind the camera, you can find me hiking in the mountains or experimenting with film photography.
